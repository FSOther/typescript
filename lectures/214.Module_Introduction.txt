When working with TypeScript

or when using TypeScript in your projects,

you'll very often use not just TypeScript in your code.

Instead, it's quite common

to also use third-party libraries,

third-party packages you might wanna use in your project.

And, of course, you can use such libraries

in TypeScript projects as well.

However, there are a couple of potential problems

or things to be aware of,

and therefore, in this section, we will explore

how you can use TypeScript with JavaScript libraries.

So libraries that are not written in TypeScript

will make sense of .d.ts files

and learn what those files are all about.

And we'll also explore libraries that are written

with TypeScript in mind,

and that might even be TypeScript first

and really only work in TypeScript projects.
